About Us

At first, we were just ordinary people that were ecstatic about chicken. We wanted to have everything to do with chicken. We loved especially fried chicken. But the problem was, fried chicken had such a bad reputation. Sure people liked it, but when people heard 'Fried chicken', they thought unhealthy, oily, fatty standards. In part, this was true! Everywhere we went, be it Burger King, McDonald's, Wendy's, Chick-Fil-A, KFC, or Popeye's, they all supported that stereotype and proved it to be true! So we, the founders of ChickStrips, set out on a long journey to create a fried chicken experience so divine, so heavenly, so holy, that the one's first experience will leave him or her coming back again and again, begging for more. That is exactly what we have created! Just like Eminem saved rap, and Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, and Hopsin are saving it, we, ChickStrips, Ansh Gandhi, Aditya Koneru, Thomas Grow, and Evan Shi, are saving fried chicken.
